Chapter 2. Normalization

The IRIs listed below attach to normalizations that should be understood as broadly and therefore subjectively defined. Such declarations are not meant to help restore an original source, but to alert users as to what wholesale alterations have been made to a source.

Table 2.1. Normalization IRIs

IRIdesc
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:hyphens-discretionary-removedDiscretionary word-break line-end hyphens have been deleted.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:space-typographer-convertedGeneral Punctuation spaces (U+2000..U+200B) to regular space have been replaced with regular space. Equivalent to fn:replace('[\x{2000} \x{2001} \x{2002} \x{2003} \x{2004} \x{2005} \x{2006} \x{2007} \x{2008} \x{2009} \x{200A} \x{200B}]',' ')
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:signals-note-removedFootnote or endnote signals (frequently superscript numbers or letters) have been deleted.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:comments-editorial-removedEditorial comments have been deleted.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:pointers-reference-removedReference pointers to other texts, both internal (cross-references) and external (citations of primary or secondary sources) have been deleted.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:milestones-reference-removedReference milestones such as page numbers and section numbers have been deleted.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:ligatures-convertedAll ligatures have been converted into constituent letters.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:letters-combining-convertedAll combining letters (U+0363..U+036F) have been converted to their corresponding ASCII counterpart.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:orthography-correctedAll orthography (spelling) has been tacitly corrected to standard forms.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:punctuation-correctedAll punctuation has been tacitly corrected to standard forms.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:punctuation-removedAll punctuation has been removed.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:capitalization-correctedAll letters have been tacitly capitalized according to standard forms.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:case-upper-to-lowerAll uppercase letters converted to lowercase.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:case-lower-to-upperAll lowercase letters converted to uppercase.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:music-printed-removedPrinted music has been removed.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:space-prepunctuation-correctedAll prepunctuation space has been removed.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:nfcAll non-NFC-compliant Unicode converted to normalized Unicode. Same effect as if running normalize-unicode(.) on a node.
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:html-to-tan-tHTML converted to TAN-T format
tag:textalign.net,2015:normalization:reference-numbers-suppressReference numbers suppressed
