Warning

This version is known to have significant errors and omissions. In case of contradictions (apparent or not) between the schemas and these guidelines, the greatest weight is to be given to the RELAX-NG schemas, then to these guidelines, and then to the Schematron schemas.

Warning

This appendix has been written piecemal, as different IRI vocabularies and specific IRIs have needed a place to reside until they could be better organized. TAN-minted IRIs are not to be viewed as published, and should not be used until otherwise indicated.
