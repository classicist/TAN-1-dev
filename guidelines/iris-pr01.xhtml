Introduction

The reliance of TAN upon IRIs to describe many kinds of things or concepts means that anyone who creates TAN files will need to have at hand reliable resources from which to draw upon vocabulary that already exists. This chapter provides a guide to those resources, beginning with stable, well-known vocabularies that already exist and then listing vocabulary that the Text Alignment Network has minted.

This appendix has been prepared to collect specialized IRI vocabularies recommended for use in TAN files. Listed below are TAN elements that require IRIs for concepts that are already well defined by other vocabularies.
